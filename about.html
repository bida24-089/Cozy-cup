Meet the Team

At COZY CUP, our people make the magic happen. Our baristas craft your coffee with passion, our chefs bring flair to every plate, and our front-of-house team ensures you always feel at home. Friendly, talented, and dedicated — our team is here for you.

Sustainability & Responsibility

We care about more than just great food and drinks. From compostable packaging to local sourcing, we take steps every day to reduce our footprint and uplift our community. When you choose Savour Sunshine, you support a business that gives back.

Events & Happenings

There’s always something brewing at Savour Sunshine. Join us for live music nights, art exhibitions, tasting events, and more. We’re not just a café — we’re a gathering place for creativity, connection, and celebration.

Looking Ahead

The journey doesn’t stop here. We’re expanding our menu, growing our team, and exploring new ways to brighten your day. Whether it’s your first visit or your fiftieth, we can’t wait to share what’s next with you.
